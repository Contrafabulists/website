We have started work on a new Contrafabulists project: predictions.contrafabulists.com.

We plan to track the predictions made about the future; and we'll see who's right, who's wrong, and who's committed to a certain technological vision of tomorrow.

How many years until self-driving cars? How many years until robots take our jobs? What's the history of these predictions? What can we learn from them?
